How can you join our team?

  1. Donate

    We need to raise a minimum of $150,000 per property for purchase price, renovations, and permit approvals from the city. Every little bit helps.

  2. Sign our petition.

    The City of Philadelphia makes the process of obtaining and developing uninhabited properties a series of convoluted, expensive procedures. Help us make it easier for non-profits like Building for Hope to do good work.

  3. Volunteer.

    We welcome individuals and small groups to sign up as construction and clean-up teams for our renovations. Bring your hard hat, work gloves, and water bottles. Come ready for hard yet satisfying work.
